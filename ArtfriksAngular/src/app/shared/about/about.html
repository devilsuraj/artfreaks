Our Vision

We are a Non-Commission based Online Art Gallery. Our aim is to create a global platform for artists to showcase and sell their works by connecting to art collectors directly. We wish to create a global art community for free flowing creative exchange between the artists and audience. Through this endeavour we hope to bring together artists from various cultures and countries to truly create a collection without boundaries

About Us


Shajee Mathew

Shajee Mathew is an art connoisseur who has been passionately collecting and investing in Contemporary and modern art for the past two decades. He has an expert understanding of the dizzying auction prices and the art of investment purchasing. He has been regularly putting up works for auction from his extensive private collection. He is the owner of a Print and Publishing house in Mumbai, India.

Shireen Kamboj

Shireen Kamboj is currently based in Houston, Texas, USA and is currently training as a Docent at the MFAH (Museum of Fine Arts Houston). After finishing her school from The Lawrence School, Sanawar, she went on to study at Delhi University, from where she graduated in 1989 in Economic Honours. She has always had an instinctive affinity towards the creative and visual arts and an uncanny knack of building strong kinship with creatively inclined individuals. She rarely misses an opportunity to tour a gallery or art museum anywhere in the world. She has had the opportunity to work on various projects alongside Maqbool Fida Husain, who was one of India’s the leading Contemporary artist.
email shireen@artfreaksindia.com


Anjum Siddiqui

Anjum Siddiqui is an artist who is originally from the India, but now works and lives in Toronto, Canada. After finishing her school from The Lawrence School, Sanawar, she went on to study at the Delhi College of Art – a leading Art School in New Delhi, India, from where she graduated in 1991 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Applied Arts. Although she has been working as a graphic designer and a visualiser, she has always had a passion and a keen eye for the fine arts and has continued to paint in various mediums. She has had the rare opportunity of working with and learning from Maqbool Fida Husain, who was one of India’s leading contemporary artist. Husain has been Anjum’s mentor and Guru since the tender age of 10 and over the years. She has had the privilege of being his only pupil.
email anjum@artfreaksindia.com


Susan Mathew

Susan Mathew is a graduate from Kishinchand Chellaram College, Mumbai University, India, with a major in history and economics, along with a course in travel and tourism from the same College. She is an avid reader and is her love for history that drew her towards an appreciation of Art. Susan is intrigued how art interacts with its subjects; stimulates the senses and results in complex emergent behaviours. In her words.. “art can be pleasant, soothing, exquisite or simply mind-blowing but never dull”.
